Hello, my name is Eddy Tougon, also known as Plet. I'm a young freelance 2D animator with a passion for fiction and art. I've been learning animation for several years now with the goal of becoming the best animator I can be. Welcome to my very first portfolio, entirely self-made!

About Me

. Je suis devenue animateur pour réaliser un rêve d’enfance : donner vie à des histoires avec le dessin. Pour concrétiser ce rêve, j’ai décidé d’apprendre l’animation 2D, une discipline qui a fini par me passionner. Cela fait maintenant trois ans que je me forme, développe mes compétences afin de devenir meilleur chaque jour.

. I became an animator to fulfill a childhood dream: bringing stories to life through drawing. To make this dream come true, I decided to learn 2D animation—a discipline that eventually became a true passion. It’s been three years now since I started training, constantly developing my skills and striving to become better every day.
